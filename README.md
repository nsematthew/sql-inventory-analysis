# SQL Inventory Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes inventory and sales data for T.T. Inc. to identify opportunities for inventory optimization, product availability improvement, and sales performance enhancement.

## Business Problem

The Supply Chain Management team required insights to:

* Optimize inventory levels
* Reduce overstock and understock situations
* Understand seasonal sales trends
* Evaluate the impact of promotions
* Improve product availability and customer satisfaction

## Tools Used

* PostgreSQL
* SQL
* pgAdmin

## Analysis Performed

The analysis explored:

* Total units sold per product SKU
* Top 10 best selling products
* Product categories with the highest sales volume
* Impact of promotions on sales quantity
* Relationship between inflation and sales volume
* GDP impact on sales performance
* Seasonal factors influencing product demand
* Product category performance comparisons

## Key Findings

* Electronics recorded the highest average sales quantity.
* Home Appliances generated the highest sales volume in November 2022.
* Promotions had limited impact on overall sales performance.
* Seasonal factors showed weak influence on sales quantities.
* Economic indicators such as GDP and inflation showed no strong direct relationship with sales volume.

## Recommendations

* Reassess promotion strategies and targeting.
* Improve demand forecasting using additional variables.
* Use actual sales patterns rather than assumptions when planning inventory.
* Monitor high performing SKUs closely to maintain availability.
